Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-04

1. Prohibited Content

You may not use OTOMOSTYLE to generate, upload, or share:

  • NSFW (Not Safe For Work), sexually explicit, or pornographic content
  • Content depicting minors in any inappropriate or sexualized manner
  • Deepfakes, face-swapping, or content intended to impersonate others
  • Hate speech, harassment, or content promoting violence
  • Illegal content or content promoting illegal activities

2. Content Moderation

All prompts are screened through automated content moderation before image generation. Prompts flagged by our moderation system will be rejected. Attempts to bypass moderation will result in account suspension.

3. Enforcement

We reserve the right to:

  • Reject any prompt or image
  • Suspend or terminate accounts that violate this policy
  • Report illegal content to appropriate authorities

4. Reporting

To report violations, contact: abuse@otomostyle.cc
