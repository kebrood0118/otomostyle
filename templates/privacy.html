Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-04

1. Information We Collect

When you register an account, we collect your username and email address. When you upload a photo for conversion, the image is processed by our AI service provider (Replicate) and deleted from our servers immediately after processing.

2. Payment Information

All payments are processed securely by Creem, our payment provider. We do not store your credit card or payment details. Creem's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.

3. Cookies

We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in and remember your language preference. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Replicate — AI image processing
  • Creem — Payment processing

5. Data Retention

Uploaded images are deleted immediately after conversion. Account data is retained until you request deletion.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: support@otomostyle.cc
